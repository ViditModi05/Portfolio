ℹ️ Description

Light and Shadow Escape is a 2D platformer puzzle game that revolves around duality. The player can switch between Light and Shadow forms, each having unique abilities to traverse obstacles and solve puzzles. With a stylized art direction and thoughtful level design, the game explores themes of contrast, balance, and strategic transformation.

✅ Contributions

  • Developed the dual-form switching mechanic with gameplay impact.
  • Designed levels to emphasize the strengths and weaknesses of each form.
  • Created shaders for light/shadow visual effects.
  • Made Finite State Machine to handle the various states and for responsive controls
  • Managed audio transitions and background score based on game state
