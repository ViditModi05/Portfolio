ℹ️ Description

Office Hours is a time-loop puzzle game where you play as an office worker who just got fired. As you pack up and try to leave, you find yourself stuck in a loop, returning to the same moment before your termination. Across 5 workdays, the office subtly shifts, objects go missing, passwords block your way, and reality distorts. But no matter what you do, escape is not that simple.
